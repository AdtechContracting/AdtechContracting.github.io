About Us

Adtech Contracting Pty Ltd is a leading telecommunications contracting company based in Bendigo, Victoria. We provide end-to-end solutions for telecommunications infrastructure and equipment, including the NBN network, developer pit and pipe work, and carrier licensing.
We serve clients across Victoria and parts of Southern New South Wales, delivering high-quality products and standards that set us apart from the rest. With 17 years of experience and a team of about 25 staff members, we are an owner-operated company that can handle any project from start to finish.
Adtech Contracting is your trusted partner for all your telecommunications needs.

Accreditation

Our work crews are composed of trained and supervised employees who have years of cable hauling and civil excavation experience under the guidance of our experienced crew leaders. They are proficient in working with both copper and fibre networks throughout Victoria, covering the Greater Melbourne & Geelong areas, as well as country Victoria. We can adapt the size of our work crews to suit the type and size of each project, ensuring that no job is too large or too small for us.
